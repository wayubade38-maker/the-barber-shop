Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information when you place an order, including: name, email, shipping address, and payment details (processed securely by Stripe).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Comply with UK legal obligations

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures. Payment data is handled exclusively by Stripe (PCI-DSS compliant). We do not store credit card information.

4. Cookies

Our site uses essential cookies for cart functionality. We do not use tracking cookies without consent.

5. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent

6. Contact Us

For privacy-related requests, email: info@thebarbershopyeovil.co.uk
